## 5. Trigonometry

Given a vector $\vec{A}$ with:
* Magnitude: $|\vec{A}| = 15$
* Angle: $\theta = 60^\circ$

We need to calculate the horizontal ($A_x$) and vertical ($A_y$) components.

---

### Solution Steps

The components are calculated using trigonometric functions:

1. **Horizontal component ($A_x$):**
   $$A_x = |\vec{A}| \cos(\theta)$$
   $$A_x = 15 \cdot \cos(60^\circ) = 15 \cdot 0.5 = 7.5$$

2. **Vertical component ($A_y$):**
   $$A_y = |\vec{A}| \sin(\theta)$$
   $$A_y = 15 \cdot \sin(60^\circ) = 15 \cdot \frac{\sqrt{3}}{2} \approx 15 \cdot 0.866 \approx 12.99$$

> **Results:**
> * Horizontal component $A_x = 7.5$
> * Vertical component $A_y \approx 12.99$
